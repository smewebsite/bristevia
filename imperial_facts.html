I. ETHNIC GROUPS AND LANGUAGES

Bristevia is home to seven major ethnic groups, with significant mixing in central and coastal provinces due to administration and trade.

Major Ethnic Groups & Associated Languages

  • Stepharian – Stepharian (Central origin, widespread via trade/administration)
  • Orlencian – Orlencian (Western mountainous roots, mining/forest trade)
  • Coastrian – Coastrian (Coastal and maritime communities)
  • Ronjian – Ronjian (Highland clans, northern migration)
  • Northavian – Northavian (Predominantly northern provinces)
  • Luminarian – Luminarian (Eastern river and coast communities)
  • Solarian – Solarian (Southern plains and islands, agriculture/maritime trade)

Official Language

  • Name: Bristevian
  • Origin: Based on the **Stepharian dialect** spoken in the Central Region (Stephara and Jaybrienne).
  • Standardization: Codified over centuries, it is the standard language of the Imperial Assembly, courts, administration, and education.
  • **Regional Variants:** Local dialects still exist, but Bristevian remains the sole official language for all state business.

II. CURRENCY AND ECONOMY

Bristevia Currency (The Bri)

  • **Name (Singular/Plural):** Bri (pronounced “bra-ay”) / Bris
  • **Subunit:** Brisets (optional for minor transactions)
  • **Symbol:** ₿ (Proposed unique Bristevian emblem)
  • **Conversion:** 1 Bri ≈ 3.80 USD (conversion rate provided for context)
  • **Denominations:**
    • **Coins:** 1 ₿, 5 ₿, 10 ₿, 20 ₿
    • **Banknotes:** 50 ₿, 100 ₿, 500 ₿, 1,000 ₿
  • **Banking & Finance:** The **Central Bank of Bristevia** issues Bris and regulates the financial system. Major commercial banks operate in Stephara City, Shadowfen, Solara, and Luminaris.

Major Industries by Region

  • **Central (Stephara, Jaybrienne):** Administrative hub, commerce, banking, light manufacturing, river trade.
  • **West (Orlencia, Westrion, etc.):** **Mining** (iron, tin, silver), timber, shipbuilding, wetland agriculture, coastal trade.
  • **North (Northaven, Montelune, etc.):** **Timber**, fur trade, mountain minerals, cold-weather agriculture.
  • **East (Orinthal, Aquaris, etc.):** **Maritime trade**, fishing, artisan industries, shipping, island ports.
  • **South (Solara, Velandor, etc.):** **Agriculture** (grain, tropical crops), coastal trade, ports, tourism.

Trade & Commerce

Bristevia's centralized economy relies on the Bri currency and an extensive infrastructure network.

  • **Exports:** Minerals, timber, seafood, agricultural produce, ships.
  • **Imports:** Luxury goods, foreign technology.

III. INFRASTRUCTURE AND TECHNOLOGY

Roads & Transportation Network

The Imperial road system ensures efficient governance, trade, and military movement across the five regions.

  • **Central National Highway (Black Road):** The primary north-south artery connecting Solara City → Stephara City → North regions. Includes the key bridge linking Thalindor City and Stephara City.
  • **Regional Highways (Blue Roads):** Connect provincial capitals to the Central National Highway.
  • **Provincial Roads (Red Roads):** Local roads connecting towns and major cities within a province to their respective provincial capitals.

Technology & Science

  • **Scientific Institutions:** Imperial Academy (Stephara City), Maritime School (Coastria, Aquaris), Northern Mineral Research Institutes.
  • **Innovation Focus:** Advanced shipbuilding and navigation, mining and metallurgy techniques, and renewable energy trials in coastal and southern regions.
